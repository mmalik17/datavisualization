MONITORING BUSINESS PERFORMANCE OF NORTHWIND TRADERS
WITH MICROSOFT POWER BI

This project is made for the submission of Maven Analytics Challenge with Northwind Traders dataset.

In this project, I explored the Northwind Traders dataset to gain meaningful insight about this company activity. The core section of this project covers several common aspects about company performance, such as sales and shipping cost trend, product performance, and top customers. The left section summarizes the top 5 most important aspects, while the right section highlights the coverage of the sales with mapping and treemaps chart.

In addition to that, I also add slicer button at the top right of the dashboard. This slicer could be used for filtering the time, place, and product aspects. With this dashboard, it could be more efficient for company executive to monitor the business performance.

The pbix file and all dataset needed could be found from the button below.

RENEWABLE ENERGY TRANSITION
IN EUROPEAN REGION

This visualization dashboard is created based on the data from Ember climate organization that I found from MakeOver Monday challenge. The visualization that I make is modified version from the visualization that made by Ember company. It shows the trend of renewable energy generation in Europe, country ranking, and portion of energy source generation.

The main insight is during 2010-2020, the portion/share of renewable energy generation in Europe is getting higher while the fossil fuel generation is getting lower. Beside showing the data over Europe, I also provide the data of trend and portion in every single country in Europe. To access that, just click the dropdown feature below the word ‘Area’.

SALES ANALYSIS DASHBOARD
OF US SUPERSTORE

The dataset that I used is US Superstore dataset, which is just a virtual data. This project has a purpose to analyze sales performance from 2014-2017 in the United States. Several insights that could be gain from this dataset are:
1. Pattern of sales history from 2014-2017
2. Sales performance by Location and Product

To add interactive feature in this dashboard, I create a filter by year, location, and also the product category. I also create menu bar so the user could switch the dashboard easily.
